Camel

Interesting Fact About Camel:

1. Camels are social animals who roam the deserts in search of food and water with up to 30 other individuals.

2. Asian camels have two humps whereas Arabian camels only have one.

3. The amount of water a camel drinks on a day-to-day basis can vary greatly, as they drink to replace only the fluid they’ve lost. A thirsty camel can drink up to 135 liters in one sitting!

Chameleons

Interesting Fact About Chameleons:

1. Their feet work like salad tongs

2. Almost half of all known species live in Madagaascar

3. Chameleons vary wildly in terms of size.

Pigeon

Interesting Fact About Pigeon:

1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.

2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.

3. Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time.

Teddy Bear

Interesting Fact About Bear:

1. Bears grieve deeply for others.

2. Bears are extraordinarily intelligent animals.

3. Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing.

Panda

Interesting Fact About Panda:

1. Their eyes are different to normal bears.

2. Cubs are well protected in their first month.

3. They have great camouflage for their environment.

Ciciada

Interesting Fact About Ciciada

1. Females may be attracted to the sound of motors.

2. Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.

3. The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
